How It Works

Four simple steps to better quiz documents.

1

Install the Extension

Add Blackboard Print to Chrome from the Chrome Web Store. No account or setup required.

2

Navigate to Your Quiz

Open any Blackboard Ultra quiz, and start a new attempt.

3

Click & Configure

Click the extension icon, choose your options (compact mode, one per page, etc.), and hit Preview.

4

Print or Save

Use your browser's print dialog to save as PDF or send directly to your printer.
